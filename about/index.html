About

I am a data geek passionate about machines & humans.

I love interacting with people and data, and have a passion for effecting change and enabling decision making. I am an energetic, curious engineer by training and self-learning, and thrive on working in diverse, cross-functional teams.

My main areas of focus now are:

  • tooling and processes for end to end data pipelines from data producers to data consumers within an organization.

Last recent years:

  • Used machine learning to recommend the fraction of future orders to be placed with vendors in each size, based on client demand and inventory dynamics.

  • Worked with business partners to connect planning to engineering data flow and allow for successive versions of algorithms to be quickly integrated.
